A little puzzle I designed for Square's 2016 CTF. I was inspired by DefCon's badge puzzles (my variant should however be significantly easier to solve).

Want to try it out? Reach out to me and I can mail it out.

Some info

  • Feel free to abuse the hardware; it's yours.
  • The board is easy to power from a wide range of sources. Don't go over ~35V.
  • Solving the puzzle may requrie using sharp or hot tools (knives, soldering iron, etc.). Handle such tools with care.

Hazardous substances

  • The board is lead-free. It was printed thru Oshpark.
  • The components were choosen to be RoHS compliant, lead free. They were purchased on Digikey
  • I used a variety of different solder. Unfortunately, most solder contains lead. Keep out of reach from children.
