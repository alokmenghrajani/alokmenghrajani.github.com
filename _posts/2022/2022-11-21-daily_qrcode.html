Today, I launched https://da.ilyqrco.de/, a website which shows a different QR Code every day. Scan the QRCode with your phone's camera app to visit a daily website I consider interesting.

I pick the best content by browsing a select few subreddits, Hackernews, and various other sources. My goal is to surprise you with an interesting mix of technology, science, and fun content.

Enjoy!
