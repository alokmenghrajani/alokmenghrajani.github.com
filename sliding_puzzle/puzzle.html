Alok's Sliding Puzzle

A sliding puzzle with a Rubik's cube-like feel.

Instructions:
  • This is a block moving puzzle. Blocks wrap around.
  • Using mouse, touch, or keyboard: slide the blocks on the larger board to match the smaller board (keyboard keys: '1', '2', '3', '4', '5', '6', 'T', 'G', 'B', 'Y', 'H', 'N' and 'shift').

Source code: sliding_puzzle
