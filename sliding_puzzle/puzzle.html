Alok's Sliding Puzzle
A sliding puzzle with a Rubik's cube-like feel

Instructions:
  • This is a block moving puzzle. Blocks wrap around.
  • Using mouse, touch, or keyboard: slide the blocks on the larger board to match the smaller board (keyboard keys: '1', '2', '3', '4', '5', 'T', 'G', 'B' and 'shift').
